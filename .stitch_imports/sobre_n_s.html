A NOSSA HISTÓRIA

Everything starts with the wood. Holm oak and almond branches, hand-gathered from the rugged hills of the Algarve. We don't use gas. We don't use electric grills. We use FOGO.

In 1984, our grandfather Manuel set up a simple brick pit outside Faro. He didn't have a menu; he had whatever the local farmers brought him and the catch of the day from the Atlantic. He understood that heat isn't just a way to cook—it's a way to transform.

Today, FOGO & BRASA carries that same primal energy to the heart of Porto. We are a Portuguese family dedicated to the ritual of the char. Our kitchen is loud, hot, and honest. We don't hide behind sauces; we let the smoke tell the story of the ingredient.
